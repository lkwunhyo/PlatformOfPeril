README
Platform Of Peril is a game with a where a hero must reach the final exit of a dungeon.
Try to reach the end, collecting all treasures, and without dying!
The program is written in C and uses the CAB202 ZDK libraries given from QUT CAB202.

HOW TO
LeftKey: Press once to walk. Press twice to run.
RightKey: Press once to walk. Press twice to run.
UpKey: Press once to jump.

Reach the exit to continue to the next level. Avoid the monsters and collect the keys 
to clear the barriers.
